About me


I’m a full-stack developer who carries a camera and a notebook. My days are split between building features and framing moments — and I love how both need patience, a little curiosity, and repetition.

I started coding because I liked making tools that helped people; I started taking pictures because I liked to remember what a light felt like. Over the years I’ve worked on web applications, automation scripts, and UX improvements — while also learning how to tell short stories through images and lines. When I’m not debugging or framing, I read essays, experiment with street photography, and collect small travel notes.
